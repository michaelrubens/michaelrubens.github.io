**Michael Rubens** is an Assistant Professor of Economics at UCLA. He works in the fields of industrial organization and labor economics. 

His research agenda relates to market power in vertical relations, with a focus on understanding the drivers, consequences, and measurement of monopsony and oligopsony power.
